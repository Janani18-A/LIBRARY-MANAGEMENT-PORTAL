GCE Library User Guide

Your comprehensive guide to accessing and utilizing all library resources and services effectively

User Orientation & Account Management

1

View Books & Information

Browse our extensive collection of 5000+ books across all engineering departments. View book details, availability status, and location information without needing to login.

2

Create Your Account

Sign up using your GCE student name and student/faculty ID. Your account gives you access to borrowing privileges, digital resources, and personalized services.

3

Book Borrowing Process

Visit the library with your registration number. Our admin will issue books through the admin portal. You can track all your borrowed books in your personal dashboard.

4

Dashboard & Notifications

Access your personal dashboard to view issued books, due dates, and fine amounts.

Borrowing Policies & Rules

Loan Periods

Students: 2 weeks (2 books max)
Faculty: 1 month (5 books max)
Staff: 3 weeks (3 books max)

Renewal Policy

Books can be renewed once if no reservations exist. Renew online through your dashboard or visit the library counter.

Search & Discovery

1

Search by Title

Enter the complete or partial title of the book. Use quotation marks for exact phrase matching.

2

Search by Author

Find all books by specific authors. Use last name first for better results (e.g., "Tanenbaum Andrew").

3

Department Filter

Browse books by specific engineering departments - Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, etc.

4

Advanced Search

Combine multiple criteria - publication year, ISBN, keywords, and availability status for precise results.

Library Infrastructure & Environment

  • Spacious Reading Hall - Comfortable seating for students with natural lighting
  • Digital Zone - 50+ computers with high-speed internet access
  • Silent Study Area - Designated quiet zone for focused individual study
  • Reference Section - Encyclopedias, dictionaries, and rare collections
  • Periodical Section - Latest journals, magazines, and newspapers
  • Wi-Fi Connectivity - Campus-wide high-speed wireless network

Academic Support & Examination Resources

Exam Preparation

Access previous years' question papers, model answers, and subject-specific study materials for semester exams.

Project References

Comprehensive collection of project reports, thesis documents, and research papers for final year projects.

Reference Books

High-demand textbooks available for in-library use only. Cannot be issued to ensure availability for all students.

Research Assistance

Get help from librarians for literature surveys, citation management, and research paper writing.
